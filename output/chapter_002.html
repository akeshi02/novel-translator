Chapter 2

The Goddess of Jiangnan University

Jiangnan University's main gate, during the time when students were leaving school, they all exited from the gate one after another.

Lin Feng could see a long-haired girl standing at the entrance of Jiangnan University's main gate from afar.

The girl had a beautiful face and was wearing a fitted dark blue dress, which highlighted her gentle and refined气质.

The soft light of the evening sunset outlined her beautiful curves, causing the male students entering and exiting the gate to cast glances in her direction.

A faint smile appeared on Lin Feng's face.

The girl dressed in a long skirt at the main gate was his girlfriend Li Siyu.

She must have been waiting for him.

Li Siyu didn't see Lin Feng because her gaze was always looking towards inside the campus.

Lin Feng wanted to quietly approach and give Li Siyu a surprise.

He put the small box given by the old man into his pocket, adjusted his clothes, then held flowers in his hands and prepared to walk toward Li Siyu.

But at this moment, a dark blue BMW drove out of the school, stopped right in front of Li Siyu.

Li Siyu's face immediately lit up with happiness and waved her hand towards the person in the car.

Lin Feng stood rooted on the spot, his body suddenly stiffened. He had an uneasy feeling in his heart.

The BMW window rolled down, and a handsome male figure sat in the driver's seat.

Lin Feng recognized him instantly - it was Zhao Feiyun, a freshman from the piano department, known as the "Piano Prince."

Zhao Feiyun came from a business family in Jiangnan City. He had money, looked good, and played the piano well. He was the ideal白马王子 for many female students at Jiangnan University.

Because of Zhao Feiyun's excellent conditions, he never lacked girlfriends.

Lin Feng didn't expect that Li Siyu was waiting not for him but for Zhao Feiyun.

Zhao Feiyun smiled and said to Li Siyu, "Siyu, you've been waiting a long time. Get in the car, I have reserved seats at the Hilton Hotel."

Li Siyu sweetly smiled and nodded gently to Zhao Feiyun, "Okay."

Then, her slender figure quickly walked over to the other side. Lin Feng saw Zhao Feiyun reach out from the car to open the door for her.

Li Siyu got in. She brushed back the hair that fell in front of her forehead, but at the next moment, she noticed Lin Feng standing several meters away.

"What's wrong?" Zhao Feiyun naturally also saw Lin Feng and knew he was Li Siyu's boyfriend.

But he pretended not to know anything, looking at Li Siyu with a playful expression and asked.

Li Siyu's facial expression almost instantly returned to normal and said, "Nothing, let's go."

Zhao Feiyun smiled satisfied. Since he could get Li Siyu into his BMW, he had also investigated Lin Feng's background.

His parents were farmers, and he was an ordinary poor boy with no background.

So Li Siyu choosing him was nothing surprising to Zhao Feiyun.

After all, people always go upward, water flows downward.

Although he only treated Li Siyu as a temporary affair, taking what he wanted from her...

Zhao Feiyun's contemptuous smile and Li Siyu's cold and indifferent gaze towards him really hurt Lin Feng's self-esteem.

He recalled the words the old man had said on his way.

Lin Feng was stunned like being struck by thunder.

He didn't think everything had been predicted by the old man.

His girlfriend had truly betrayed him.

Anger filled his heart.

Could Li Siyu's previous purity, her lack of vanity and love for money all be fake?

The BMW drove away. Zhao Feiyun wasn't leaving; instead, he directly stopped the car next to Lin Feng.

There was no need to say it was intentional.

He looked at Lin Feng with a smile: "Lin Feng, is that flower in your hand meant for Siyu? But sorry, she's now my girlfriend."

He took Lin Feng's girlfriend and didn't forget to mock him.

Arrogant, boastful, looking down on others, unbearable!

This was Zhao Feiyun.

Relying on his family's wealth and power, he had no regard for Lin Feng at all.

Facing Zhao Feiyun's humiliation, Lin Feng remained unusually calm and countered: "Zhao公子, you're wrong. This flower isn't meant for her. But Zhao公子 likes the women I've used before, your appetite is quite good."

"What did you say?" Zhao Feiyun immediately became furious.

Faced with Zhao Feiyun's anger, Lin Feng showed no emotion at all.

Zhao Feiyun wasn't trying to humiliate him?

Now let him know what it means to bring disgrace upon himself.

"Lin Feng, what are you talking about? I've never liked you. Don't be foolishly sentimental, I'm Zhao Feiyun's girlfriend!" Li Siyu was very nervous, fearing that Zhao Feiyun would misunderstand, and shouted angrily at Lin Feng.

Her shout caused the students leaving school to stop and look in this direction.

Lin Feng almost couldn't believe his ears.

Li Siyu had just said she had never liked him and called him foolishly sentimental.

Moreover, she intentionally made it loud for everyone around to know that she was Zhao Feiyun's girlfriend.

This was clearly trying to cut off her relationship with Lin Feng and show loyalty to Zhao Feiyun?

Absolutely!

She did it very well.

She gave Lin Feng no face at all.

It was as if they were two different people - the simple, pure Li Siyu he had known before.

This was obviously a heartless person!

Lin Feng sneered: "Oh, she's a heartless person. I really blindfolded myself!"

He didn't even have the interest to look at Li Siyu's face anymore; he just turned around and left directly.

"Hey, poor boy, find a girl, give her these flowers, maybe she'll like you, ha ha..." A mocking laugh came from behind Lin Feng.

Lin Feng's anger boiled over.

Zhao Feiyun's words were clearly mocking him as a poor boy who was so naive to believe Li Siyu would like him?

At this moment, Lin Feng saw several girls coming out of the school gate.

Leading them was a girl with long flowing hair. Her beautiful face looked like an artwork by a master, perfect and exquisite.

Her skin was as white as snow, delicate and tender, her eyes clear and bright, and when she smiled, it was like two charming crescent moons. Her figure was slender and her curves were enchanting. Every part of her exuded the charm of a goddess.

The attention of all the male students leaving school was immediately drawn to this girl who looked like a goddess.

Lin Feng was stunned for a while before he came back to his senses. He felt that this beautiful girl seemed familiar.

The humiliation from Zhao Feiyun and Li Siyu made Lin Feng make a decision in his heart.

He stepped forward quickly, and with the surprised gazes of all the students around him, he suddenly handed the bouquet in his hand to this beautiful girl.

The girl's beautiful eyes were filled with surprise and confusion.

Her companions nearby couldn't help covering their mouths in amazement: What was going on?

At the moment when everyone was stunned, Lin Feng didn't wait for the beautiful girl to react. He immediately embraced her.

The girl was like a startled rabbit, completely unable to make any reaction.

"I like you!" Lin Feng whispered in the girl's ear.

Then he leaned down and lightly kissed her face, released her, and left with an indifferent and arrogant demeanor.

His carefree figure carried an air of dominance...

Then, the girls around him came back to their senses, looking at the girl who had been kissed with disbelief: "Chen Jiayi, is this guy confessing to you?"

"We're our big beauty being kissed?"

"What if our big beauty gets pregnant after being kissed?"

Chen Jiayi's face immediately turned red. Although she was also surprised and even a bit angry that Lin Feng had kissed her.

But Chen Jiayi said nothing, hurriedly left, and the other girls didn't expect Chen Jiayi to say nothing, so they rushed after her.

Inside the BMW, Zhao Feiyun and Li Siyu couldn't believe their eyes.

Especially for Zhao Feiyun, it was a shock. Lin Feng had confessed to Chen Jiayi in front of everyone and even kissed her?

Chen Jiayi's existence at Jiangnan University was something he knew well.

No matter her beauty or her family's strength, they were beyond his reach as a rich second-generation.

He didn't even have the qualification to touch her fingers.

But Lin Feng, this poor boy, had dared to embrace Chen Jiayi in public and kissed her.

This made Zhao Feiyun feel like he had been hit by thousands of blows, jealous to the point of madness.

Li Siyu also couldn't believe what she saw. In her eyes, Lin Feng was now worthless.

He had confessed to and kissed the most beautiful woman at Jiangnan University, Chen Jiayi in front of her?

This was a slap in the face!
