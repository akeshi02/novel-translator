Chapter 8

Next Winning Numbers

"Wu wu wu......"

The sound of a police siren echoed, as two police cars sped toward from the distant road.

At the same time, from the opposite direction on the road, there came the barking of a small dog "wang wang wang", along with the footsteps of several people running towards.

Lin Feng saw that Xiao Xueqiu was running towards them at maximum speed, while behind it ran four or five tall bodyguards.

Indeed, the little snowball had run back to the villa to get help earlier.

The guards in the villa had only seen Xiao Xueqiu return but not Ye Xuerou. The small dog was restless and barking uncontrollably, even biting their pants trying to pull them out.

They then realized that Ye Xuerou had been attacked.

Therefore, several bodyguards followed Xiao Xueqiu to search for Ye Xuerou.

About ten meters away from Lin Feng and Ye Xuerou, the guards began pointing at Lin Feng and coldly shouting:

"Kid, quickly put down our little daughter!"

"Do not be rude to our little daughter!"

Without a doubt, they had mistaken Lin Feng, who was holding Ye Xuerou, for a villain, thinking that Lin Feng wanted to commit some wrongdoing against Ye Xuerou.

Lin Feng: ...

Ye Xuerou saw her bodyguards misunderstood Lin Feng and hurriedly whispered to Lin Feng, "Quickly put me down..."

Being held by Lin Feng in front of her own guards, she felt a bit embarrassed.

Lin Feng was holding a beautiful woman, with the faint fragrance of a beauty on his nose. He couldn't help but feel a sense of enjoyment, but since the police car was about to arrive and Ye Xuerou's bodyguards had come, he didn't need to hold her anymore.

Therefore, Lin Feng put Ye Xuerou down.

The guards quickly rushed forward, wanting to arrest Lin Feng as this "villain."

Ye Xuerou immediately stopped them, saying, "He is not a villain. He is my savior. The one who wanted to harm me was Zhao Yunfei."

At this point, Ye Xuerou pointed toward the nearby Zhao Yunfei, who was lying on the ground howling in pain.

The guards then realized they had misunderstood Lin Feng and immediately rushed forward to take Zhao Yunfei down.

Zhao Yunfei cried out in agony again, suffering terribly.

At this moment, two police cars arrived before everyone, with seven officers getting off the vehicles.

A middle-aged officer took the lead, looking at the bodyguards and the captured Zhao Yunfei, asking, "What exactly happened? Who reported the crime?"

"I did." Lin Feng stepped forward and said.

"You claimed someone committed murder. What's going on?" asked the middle-aged officer.

Lin Feng then played the video of Zhao Yunfei chasing Ye Xuerou on his phone for the middle-aged officer to see.

After watching, the middle-aged officer's expression immediately became serious, and he told his two officers beside him, "Take the suspect back."

"Yes, Chief." The two officers stepped forward, handcuffed Zhao Yunfei, and took him onto the police car.

Zhao Yunfei called out repeatedly, "I'm innocent! I'm innocent..."

The middle-aged officer coldly smiled, "Whether you're innocent or not will be clear once you get to the police station."

Then, the middle-aged officer looked at Lin Feng and Ye Xuerou, saying, "You two also go to the police station for a statement."

Lin Feng said to the middle-aged officer, "Ye Xuerou's foot is injured. She needs treatment. Maybe I can accompany you to the police station first for the statement."

The middle-aged officer looked at Ye Xuerou's injured foot and nodded, saying, "Then I'll leave two colleagues here and ask Ye Xuerou about the details of what happened."

Ye Xuerou nodded.

The middle-aged officer, Lin Feng, and other officers got into a police car.

"Wu wu wu~~~" The police car siren sounded as it left.

Ye Xuerou looked at the departing police car, thinking about how Lin Feng had been concerned about her injured foot before leaving. Her heart seemed to follow the police car away.

"Miss Ye Xuerou, let's first take you to the hospital!" said one officer with concern.

Ye Xuerou came back to her senses and shook her head at the officer, saying, "My foot is only slightly hurt; it's not a big deal. Let me go to my villa so that my private doctor can treat it."

"Okay, then let's go to Miss Ye Xuerou's villa." The officer nodded and opened the back door of the police car for her.

With the help of another officer, Ye Xuerou got into the police car.

The two officers sat in the front seats, and the police car started moving toward Ye Xuerou's villa.

Meanwhile, Ye Xuerou's bodyguards and Xiao Xueqiu ran after the police car to the villa.

After more than an hour, Lin Feng finished his statement and left the police station.

Looking at the time, it was already over 11:00 in the afternoon. He found a roadside restaurant to have lunch.

After eating, he returned to the hotel.

On his way back to the hotel, he passed by a sports lottery shop and suddenly heard a loud ringing sound in his ears.

Lin Feng stopped immediately, thinking that something was wrong with his ears.

Then, a voice like that of a TV host echoed in his ear: "Super Big Lottery No. 20200 has been drawn; the front zone winning numbers are 15, 25, 17, 19, and 03; the back zone winning numbers are 02, 08..."

"Super Big Lottery No. 20200 has been drawn; the front zone winning numbers are... "

"Super Big Lottery No. 20200..."

This was like a live broadcast of the lottery draw, with the host announcing the winning numbers, repeating three times in Lin Feng's ears.

Moreover, the seven winning numbers for both zones were deeply imprinted into Lin Feng's mind.

Lin Feng looked toward the lottery shop, where he could see several men buying sports lottery tickets.

Inside the store, a large screen TV was hanging on the wall, playing live coverage of the lottery draw. The host was announcing the winning numbers: "Super Big Lottery No. 20199 has been drawn; the front zone winning numbers are 08, 11, 05, 01, and 22; the back zone winning numbers are 03, 12..."

Lin Feng was stunned upon seeing this: Super Big Lottery No. 20199?

Then, the previous sound he heard was for Super Big Lottery No. 20200's winning numbers, not for No. 20199. Was it the next draw's winning numbers?

After taking the old man's elixir, Lin Feng had gained the ability to foresee.

Therefore, it wasn't strange that he could predict the winning numbers of the next Super Big Lottery.

Then, Lin Feng walked into the sports lottery shop.

Inside the store, he bought a single ticket with 12 times the stake based on the winning numbers he just obtained.

After buying the lottery ticket, Lin Feng checked the draw time, which was every Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday. Today was Friday.

That meant that tomorrow he could find out whether his ticket had won or not.

Lin Feng put the purchased lottery ticket into his wallet and returned to the hotel.

He didn't check out of the hotel but decided to stay for another day, waiting until the evening draw results came out, then check out the next day to claim the prize.

Sitting on the sofa in his room, Lin Feng casually flipped through the news on his phone.

Then, he was suddenly attracted by a piece of news on his phone, which had many updates about the Ye family and the Zhao family:

"Ye Group, the largest chip company in Jiangnan City, announced today that it has cut off supply to Zhao's smart phone chips."

"Zhao's smartphones face chip supply cuts, facing bankruptcy crisis."

"Zhao Yunfei met with a woman secretly in a hotel; his girlfriend Ye Xuerou publicly announced her breakup with him."

"Zhao Yunfei retaliated against the Ye family for cutting off chip supplies, and his plot to assassinate Ye Xuerou was exposed and he was arrested by the police."

"The Ye Group has dispatched its legal team; Zhao Yunfei is likely to be convicted and imprisoned."

Lin Feng saw this and smiled with a calm expression.

Zhao Yunfei had transformed from an arrogant rich kid into a murderer, about to be sentenced to prison, never able to rise again.

Meanwhile, the once prestigious Zhao family was on the verge of collapse, in dire straits.

However, no one knew that this huge disaster for Zhao Yunfei and his family was easily caused by Lin Feng using his supernatural abilities.

Lin Feng, who had gained supernatural powers, could be considered a mastermind behind the scenes, with everything under control.

He could turn clouds into rain at will!
