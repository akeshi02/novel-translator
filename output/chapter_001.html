Chapter 1

This Old Fella's a Real Pain

In the evening in Jiangnan City, Summer Country.

Lin Feng came out of a flower shop, holding a bunch of bright roses in his arms.

Today was his girlfriend's birthday.

The flower shop was only about five or six hundred meters away from where he and his girlfriend studied at Jiangnan University. He started walking towards the university.

But before he had walked a few meters, he suddenly tripped over something, stumbled slightly, and almost fell down.

"Aiyah! That hurts!" At the same time, an old man's voice rang out.

Lin Feng found that he had stepped on the foot of an elderly man sitting by the roadside.

The old man had white hair and a white beard, his clothes were dirty and disheveled, looking like he hadn't washed himself in days.

In front of him was a stainless steel basin.

The old man was rubbing his cloth shoes with his hands, groaning in pain.

Lin Feng felt embarrassed and kept apologizing to the old man repeatedly.

Then he took out his wallet.

There were only a few red RMB notes inside. He took one out and placed it into the basin in front of the old man.

After putting the money down, Lin Feng turned around and wanted to leave.

"You don't go..." The old man suddenly spoke up.

Lin Feng stopped in his tracks, his heart skipping a beat: Would the old man try to讹 him?

He was just a poor guy; his family didn't have any mines.

"Old man, you... what else do you want?" Lin Feng's voice trembled slightly as he spoke.

"Young man, I am the 198th generation disciple of Zhongnan Mountain Quanzhen Sect, having wandered for many years. Now that I'm old, I wish to return to Zhongnan Mountain and settle down." The old man looked up at Lin Feng and said.

"Just give me another two hundred yuan; it will make up the travel expenses."

Lin Feng sighed in relief inside.

It was fortunate it was only two hundred yuan. If it were twenty thousand, he would probably have to call 110 for police help.

Although Lin Feng was also poor, he had just stepped on the old man's foot, so giving an extra two hundred yuan could be considered as buying medicine for the old man.

Sweeep.

Lin Feng took out another two hundred yuan from his wallet and placed it into the basin in front of the old man.

"Old man, two hundred." After saying that, Lin Feng wanted to leave quickly.

To avoid the old man causing any more trouble.

But at this moment, a thin, bony hand suddenly grabbed Lin Feng's right wrist.

The one who grabbed Lin Feng's wrist was the old man's hand.

Lin Feng's face changed. He asked the old man, "Old man, what now~~~"

"Young man, since you're so kind-hearted, I can't repay you. There is a small wooden box in my hand with a pill from Zhongnan Mountain; I'll give it to you as a token of gratitude." The old man handed Lin Feng a small wooden box.

"Just take this pill from the box. It will make you immune to all diseases. The spiritual energy of the pill will wash and strengthen your skin, muscles, bones, and marrow, making you an extraordinary martial artist.

When the spiritual energy opens up all your meridians in your body, you can see the great world, everything others cannot see.

All the secrets in this world will be as clear to you as a mirror."

Lin Feng's lips trembled slightly.

This old man was definitely a charlatan!

Zhongnan Mountain pill?

Eating it would make one an extraordinary martial artist?

Why don't you say you're the master of Quanzhen Sect, Wang Chongyang?

Or that you can see things others cannot?

Why don't you say you're a god?

I'd believe you if I had a ghost!

"Young man, you don't believe me?" Seeing Lin Feng's unimpressed look, the old man said seriously: "You might not know that something big is about to happen to you?"

Lin Feng didn't believe it: "Then, old man, what will happen to me?"

"You're going to have a huge piece of grassland on your head soon." The old man looked at Lin Feng's head and said in an old-fashioned manner.

Lin Feng realized this. He was somewhat angry and said to the old man: "You... you cursed me to wear a green hat? You old bastard is really bad!"

Right, now if someone says you have a huge piece of grassland on your head, it means they're saying you've been cheated on.

How could Lin Feng not be angry?

This old man's heart was really rotten.

But the old man laughed: "I cursed you? I didn't curse you. Your girlfriend is about to break up with you soon. However, the old must make way for the new, and there will be a big piece of peach blossom waiting for you soon.

Whether it's peach blossom luck or peach blossom misfortune depends on your fate."

Lin Feng heard this and just wanted to laugh: "Old man, what kind of joke are you playing? My relationship with my girlfriend is great. How could she break up with me?"

As for the old man saying there was a big piece of peach blossom waiting for him, it was sheer nonsense.

The old man didn't explain further, released Lin Feng's wrist, and waved his hand.

"Go quickly, or your girlfriend will leave with a rich young master."

"Old man, I'll be back right away!" Lin Feng said angrily and rushed towards Jiangnan University.

He wanted to bring Li Siyu over so the old man could see how good their relationship was!

You old bastard, dare to curse me to have a huge piece of grassland on my head. Wait until I get you!
