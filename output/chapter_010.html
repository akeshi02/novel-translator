Chapter 10

A Second Encounter with Chen Jiayi!

Lin Feng got into Fan Xiaodong's car, just buckled up his seatbelt when Fan Xiaodong asked him, "Lin Feng, what kind of house do you want to buy? How big and at what price?"

"Several hundred square meters, a standalone villa, costing about one or two million." Lin Feng thought for a moment before saying.

After all, he had over a billion in his possession.

Buying a standalone villa for one or two million was not unreasonable!

Even if the villa was several hundred square meters, it would be too big for him and his parents and sister to live in together.

But once he had a wife and children, more people would make it feel less spacious.

Most importantly, he had an ability that others didn't: a pair of eyes that could see through life and death, misfortune and fortune, and also perceive the wealth and prosperity of the world.

With this divine eye, Lin Feng did not need to worry about money at all.

Therefore, spending one or two million on a villa was nothing for him.

However, Fan Xiaodong was shocked, his mouth wide open as if it could fit an egg in.

Several hundred square meters of a standalone villa, costing one or two million!

It sounded so light when coming out of Lin Feng's mouth, like saying nothing at all.

Even someone born into a wealthy merchant family couldn't be that extravagant.

He felt like Lin Feng had won not just millions but billions in the lottery.

But he didn't ask any more questions.

Whoever had how much wealth, he shouldn't pry too deeply, right?

All he needed to know was that Lin Feng was now a rich guy.

Fan Xiaodong thought for a moment, his eyes lit up, and said to Lin Feng, "Got it! A friend who deals with real estate told me a few days ago. Today, Huafu Real Estate has an opening sale of a top-tier residential complex. They say all the villas are worth millions or even tens of millions. Let's go check it out!"

Huafu Real Estate was not only the largest real estate group in Jiangnan City but also a very famous real estate company throughout Xia country.

Besides that, Lin Feng knew from two days ago that Chen Jiayi, the campus flower of Jiangnan University, was the beloved daughter of Huafu Group's chairman.

"By the way, maybe this time Chen Jiayi will also come to the opening sale of Huafu Group's top-tier residential complex." Fan Xiaodong started the car while gossiping like a woman to Lin Feng, "Brother, you haven't told me yet how you managed to get close to Chen Jiayi?"

Lin Feng honestly said, "I didn't seduce her."

"What?" Fan Xiaodong hit the brake, and the car that had just started moving came to an immediate stop. He looked at Lin Feng in disbelief, "You didn't seduce Chen Jiayi? Then what happened two days ago when you kissed her after school?"

Lin Feng was a bit speechless: "...Can't you be so dramatic? Just drive, I'll tell you slowly."

After about fifteen minutes, Fan Xiaodong, having heard the whole story, looked stunned.

My god!

What kind of trashy drama is this?

He had originally thought that Lin Feng and Chen Jiayi were a romantic story of a poor boy pursuing a rich girl.

But in reality, Lin Feng was betrayed by his girlfriend's scheming heart, so he just kissed a random girl to restore his manhood, only to find out that the girl he kissed was actually Chen Jiayi.

Fan Xiaodong couldn't blame himself for not knowing the whole story. After all, Chen Jiayi was too famous.

Everyone saw Lin Feng kissing her but didn't know that Li Siyu had betrayed him.

After learning the truth, Fan Xiaodong frowned worriedly and said to Lin Feng, "You kissed Chen Jiayi. This incident has already caused a storm in Jiangnan University. Maybe you should hide for a few days outside the campus until this matter passes before returning to school?"

Lin Feng smiled: "Are you afraid that the rich guys who like Chen Jiayi will take revenge on me?"

Fan Xiaodong nodded and said, "You might not know, but there is one person who pursues Chen Jiayi. Not only are we unable to deal with him, but even other rich kids are wary of him."

"Who?" Lin Feng was naturally curious.

"Zheng William," Fan Xiaodong said seriously.

A name that's neither Chinese nor Western.

But it's a very famous name in Jiangnan University and even throughout Jiangnan City.

The Zheng family, with Meihua Group, is a giant enterprise covering many fields such as biopharmaceuticals, medical equipment, beauty, cosmetics, etc.

Their wealth is strong, comparable to the wealthy Ouyang family.

As a third-generation heir of the Zheng family, Zheng William, the eldest son of the Zheng family, was well-known in Jiangnan University and throughout Jiangnan City.

However, Lin Feng had known this name before but only just learned from Fan Xiaodong that Zheng William was also one of Chen Jiayi's suitors.

"Although not many people in Jiangnan University know that Zheng William is pursuing Chen Jiayi, the business circle almost everyone knows that the Zheng family and Ouyang family have close ties and are trying to form an alliance."

Fan Xiaodong looked at Lin Feng with concern.

"So, if Zheng William wants to do something bad to you, you won't be able to fight back against him."

Lin Feng thought in his mind: That's not necessarily true!

Now, the spiritual energy inside his body surged like a river. Even if he was an extraordinary master, he wouldn't fear Zheng William, let alone be afraid of one person.

Even more so, with his supernatural ability, the divine eyes that could foresee dangers and disasters, and see every move of the opponent, he would stand on the position of not being defeated.

But Lin Feng didn't say all this out loud. He just casually said to Fan Xiaodong: "Okay, I'll be careful."

Twenty minutes later, with a '嘎' sound, Fan Xiaodong's Land Rover stopped at the parking lot of a newly opened residential complex.

This newly launched residential complex was exactly the top-tier villa project called "Hao Ting Hua Yuan" by Huafu Real Estate.

After getting out of the car, Lin Feng saw that the parking lot was full of luxury cars, all Bentleys, Rolls-Royces, Ferraris, and Mercedes-Benzes...

It seemed this top-tier luxurious residential complex had attracted many wealthy people from Jiangnan City.

Around the parking lot stood a row of luxurious villas, elegant and grand, clearly of high class.

"So many top-tier luxury cars. It seems that many big shots have taken an interest in this residential complex," Fan Xiaodong saw so many expensive cars parked there and was somewhat worried: "Huafu Real Estate's 'Hao Ting Hua Yuan' only has 500 units for sale, with so many people competing, it's probably hard to get a house."

But Lin Feng didn't worry much. If he couldn't buy here, he could go elsewhere.

The two walked into the sales office.

The sales hall was very spacious and crowded, with voices all around.

Most of them were well-dressed middle-aged men, fat and wearing expensive watches, and some older rich women wearing expensive diamond rings, big pearl necklaces, and carrying foreign limited edition handbags.

These were all wealthy people who didn't have money problems, opening their mouths to ask for several units of hundreds of square meters of villas as if buying cabbage.

Although the sales office had dozens of staff members, they couldn't handle so many buyers, working like a spinning top.

Lin Feng saw this and felt somewhat worried. Even if he wanted to buy a villa, there was no one to consult with.

Moreover, Lin Feng had actually asked a couple of sales ladies about it, but the sales ladies looked at him as an ordinary person in his twenties, not likely to be a rich boss.

They politely let him look through the related materials for "Hao Ting Hua Yuan" and then turned back to serve those wealthy middle-aged men who opened their mouths to ask for several units of villas.

"Look down on people!" Fan Xiaodong muttered lowly.

But even with his small fortune, he felt inadequate in front of those rich old ladies who asked for several units of villa at once.

Lin Feng saw this big group of土豪s fighting over millions of villa and thought that even if it was his turn, the villas would be sold out.

So, Lin Feng decided to leave and check other residential complexes.

But just then, he saw a familiar beautiful figure walk into the sales hall.

Long flowing hair, perfect oval face with flawless skin, eyebrows like smoke, eyes like spring water and autumn moon, beautiful beyond description, lips as red as rouge.

Dressed in black OL business suit, standing tall and graceful, exuding a sense of professionalism and cleanliness of an office lady.

"Chen Jiayi has really come..." Fan Xiaodong was also surprised to see this beautiful figure.

Yes, the beautiful figure entering the sales hall was indeed Chen Jiayi.

Similarly, when she entered the sales hall, she saw Lin Feng and Fan Xiaodong.

But she didn't pay much attention to them. Instead, she ordered ten or so sales ladies from other places who had been called in to support the new residential complex's launch to immediately start working.

After assigning tasks, slightly tired Chen Jiayi found a quiet corner sofa in the sales hall and sat down.

Seeing Chen Jiayi sitting alone in the corner, Lin Feng said to Fan Xiaodong: "Wait for me here."

Then he walked towards Chen Jiayi.

Fan Xiaodong widened his eyes: What was Lin Feng going to do?
