Chapter 3

Superpower from the Elixir

Lin Feng gave the rose to Chen Jiayi in public, embraced and kissed her, but it was certainly not because he really liked her.

He even didn't know who she was?

He did this just to retaliate against Zhao Feiyun and Li Siyu.

Didn't Zhao Feiyun say that no girl would like a poor guy like him?

Then he showed him by giving the flower to a beautiful girl in front of his face, embracing and kissing her.

And this girl was even dozens times more beautiful than Li Siyu!

It made Zhao Feiyun and Li Siyu furious.

After leaving school, Lin Feng went to find the old man.

He went to find the old man because the old man had previously said that Li Siyu would betray him and fall in love with a rich young master.

And everything turned out exactly as he predicted.

The old man was clearly an extraordinary person who knew things before they happened.

But when he returned to where the old man used to sit, he found that the old man had already left.

Lin Feng felt a bit depressed: did the old man really return to Zhongnan Mountain?

He instinctively took out the small box given by the old man from his pocket.

"You just need to take this pill in my box. It can make you immune to all diseases. The spiritual energy of the pill will wash and strengthen your skin, muscles, bones, and marrow, making you an extraordinary master.

When the spiritual energy passes through all your meridians, you'll be able to see everything that others cannot see in this world.

All secrets in the world will appear clearly before your eyes like a mirror."

These were the words the old man said when giving him the pill.

He didn't believe them at first, but now he did.

But now the old man was gone, and he couldn't ask him any more questions.

Lin Feng decided to find a place to study the pill given by the old man.

After several minutes, in a budget hotel room.

Lin Feng sat on the bed and opened the small box given by the old man.

Immediately, an unusual fragrance of the pill filled his nose, making him feel refreshed and clear-headed!

Inside the small box, there was one black pill about the size of a thumb.

Lin Feng took out the pill and put it in his mouth.

Just as he was about to chew it, he couldn't believe that the pill dissolved instantly upon entering his mouth, flowing down his throat like liquid, warm and comfortable.

Finally, it flowed into his lower dantian.

Then, the peaceful dantian suddenly erupted like a storm at sea.

Boom!

Lin Feng felt a loud boom in his dantian.

He was extremely shocked, not knowing what had happened?

Then, an endless flow of energy burst out from his dantian, instantly spreading through all his meridians and limbs.

The powerful current of energy washed and strengthened his meridians, cutting away the coarse parts and refining them.

He could even feel his meridians expanding and becoming more resilient.

His bones and muscles emitted a creaking sound as they were constantly being reinforced.

After his physical meridians had been strengthened, Lin Feng then felt that all his acupoints in his body were also being opened by the energy flow one after another.

Each time an acupoint was opened, it made a popping sound, like the sound of popped corn from a bamboo tube.

When all his acupoints had been opened by the energy flow.

Boom!

Lin Feng's ears suddenly experienced a loud noise.

At once, he heard various sounds in his ears: city traffic and honking cars, waves crashing, seagulls calling, strong winds howling, eagles crying out, horse hooves on grasslands...

It seemed like all the sounds from the world had gathered together.

His ears were as if opening up a new world.

Then, his nose's sense of smell also became incredibly powerful at once.

The smell of car exhaust, the salty scent of seawater, the fragrance of flowers brought by strong winds, and the fresh air of the grasslands all appeared in his nose.

It was like his nose could detect various scents from all over the world.

However, what surprised him even more was his eyes. His pupils contracted slightly, and he suddenly saw soaring eagles in the sky, galloping horses on the grasslands, continuous mountain ranges, endless oceans...

As if he could see all the beautiful scenery of the world with a single glance.

Finally, these scenes disappeared one by one.

Then, Lin Feng returned to reality.

Lin Feng couldn't express his shock. His eyes, nose, and ears seemed to have changed.

He could now see, hear, and smell various things.

Did he really become what the old man said? After taking the pill and having the spiritual energy open all his acupoints, could he see many things that others couldn't?

Lin Feng had a lot of questions in his mind.

If he could see many things that others couldn't, then could he see what Li Siyu and Zhao Feiyun were doing right now?

As soon as this thought came to him, his pupils contracted slightly, and a miraculous scene appeared. He suddenly saw a hotel with dazzling lights and luxurious decoration.

Inside the hotel, Li Siyu and Zhao Feiyun were sitting in a private room enjoying Western cuisine.

Li Siyu and Zhao Feiyun raised their glasses and gently clinked them together.

After drinking half of the red wine, Li Siyu's face turned slightly flushed, with a bit of flirtatiousness in her eyes and eyebrows.

Zhao Feiyun put down his glass and said, "I didn't think this Lin Feng would be so bold as to insult Chen Jiayi in public. This kid is really unaware of the consequences!"

Li Siyu seemed not to want to talk about Lin Feng's incident, saying, "Feiyun, let's not mention him. Tonight is our date."

Zhao Feiyun realized and smiled meaningfully: "Right, right, let's not talk about that poor guy. Today we should enjoy our intimate world together..."

Li Siyu looked affectionately at Zhao Feiyun, coquettishly saying, "You're really bad~~~"

Lin Feng mentally cursed: an actor!

Indeed, all the purity she showed in front of him was fake!

After cursing in his mind, Lin Feng then thought about Zhao Feiyun's mention of Chen Jiayi. Why did this name feel so familiar?

Suddenly, Lin Feng snapped out of it and remembered that Chen Jiayi was actually the first campus flower at Jiangnan University.

And she came from a powerful noble family in Jiangnan City with an impressive background.

Lin Feng suddenly felt stunned. That's why he had felt somewhat familiar when seeing Chen Jiayi at the school gate before.

He had kissed the first campus flower of Jiangnan University, Chen Jiayi!

Lin Feng's heart sank as he thought: Oh no!
