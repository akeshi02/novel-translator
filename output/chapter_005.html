Chapter 5

Revenge Served Hot

Several minutes later, Ye Xuerou and her entourage arrived at the Hilton Hotel.

Ye Xuerou led over a dozen bodyguards into the hotel quickly.

The door to room 302 was kicked open violently with a loud bang.

With her phone recording on, Ye Xuerou entered the hotel confidently, accompanied by her bodyguards.

Zhang Yunfei and Li Siyu were hurriedly adjusting their clothes on the bed.

"Go ahead, don't worry about me," Ye Xuerou said to Zhang Yunfei while sitting down on the sofa, continuing to record with her phone.

Her attitude was as if she were about to do a live broadcast.

Zhang Yunfei looked flustered and embarrassed, stammering an explanation to Ye Xuerou: "Xue'er, I... I got drunk at my friend's birthday party, it was this woman who lured me!"

As if he had found an excuse to justify himself, Zhang Yunfei immediately pointed at Li Siyu.

Ye Xuerou coldly smiled and said, "Is that so? Then what about this message?"

After saying that, Ye Xuerou turned her phone screen directly toward Zhang Yunfei.

Zhang Yunfei immediately saw a text message on his phone:

"Xue'er, please don't call Zhang Yunfei anymore. He doesn't have any interest in you. Now he and I are staying at room 302 of the Hilton Hotel. A good night is worth a thousand golds. Li Siyu says goodnight to you!"

The sender was Zhang Yunfei's own phone number.

After reading this message, Zhang Yunfei felt an urge to strangle Li Siyu.

He hadn't expected that Li Siyu had used his own phone to send such a foolish message to Ye Xuerou.

That's why Ye Xuerou had brought people to storm the door.

Zhang Yunfei silently cursed in his heart: What a stupid woman!

Then, Zhang Yunfei turned around and slapped Li Siyu once.

A loud crack echoed as Li Siyu screamed in pain.

Her eyes widened in disbelief at being hit by Zhang Yunfei.

"Damn you, you cheap woman! You lured me while I was drunk, and also sent messages to my girlfriend. You intentionally made her misunderstand you. What are your intentions?" Zhang Yunfei shouted.

Li Siyu couldn't believe that the Zhang Yunfei who had just been so close to her was now acting like a stranger.

She looked at him with an expression of委屈: "Yunfei, what's wrong with you? You said you only loved me. Also, I didn't send any messages!"

She was indeed innocent!

Because this message had been sent by Lin Feng using Zhang Yunfei's phone under Li Siyu's name to Ye Xuerou.

Even if she denied sending the message, neither Zhang Yunfei nor Ye Xuerou would believe her words.

"Smack! Smack!!" Li Siyu's defense earned another two slaps from Zhang Yunfei.

"You cheap woman, who likes you? Keep talking nonsense and I'll break your mouth!" Zhang Yunfei warned angrily.

Li Siyu had a red cheek and blood was flowing from her lips.

Zhang Yunfei turned against her without mercy, treating her like a stranger, which terrified her deeply.

This made her recall how she had treated Lin Feng earlier in the afternoon to please Zhang Yunfei.

Only now, the role of Lin Feng had been transferred to her.

Zhang Yunfei's ruthless treatment of her was all for showing off to Ye Xuerou, his real girlfriend.

Li Siyu felt her heart bleeding...

Zhang Yunfei's ruthless actions toward Li Siyu were indeed a performance meant to impress Ye Xuerou.

Ye Xuerou wasn't just his girlfriend; in business matters, many aspects of the Zhao family required the Ye family's support.

Dismissing Ye Xuerou would mean that both Zhang Yunfei and the Zhao family would be doomed?

If it weren't for Ye Xuerou being so dominant, not even allowing him to touch her hand, he wouldn't have used Li Siyu to deal with his loneliness.

Who knew that getting involved with other women would lead to being caught red-handed by Ye Xuerou!

What a terrible fate!

Thus, Zhang Yunfei was in such a hurry to distance himself from Li Siyu.

After watching Zhang Yunfei's performance, Ye Xuerou stood up and applauded: "Good! So that means I misunderstood you, Zhang Yunfei."

Zhang Yunfei hurriedly stepped forward, apologizing: "Xue'er, it was all my fault for drinking too much, which made me fall for this woman..."

"Smack!" But before he could finish his sentence, Ye Xuerou slapped him on the left cheek.

The slap left his face burning with pain.

Zhang Yunfei looked surprised and only came to his senses after a while: "Xue'er, why did you...?"

But before he could say anything more, Ye Xuerou slapped him again on the right cheek.

"Acting in front of me, do you think I'm really stupid? Once unfaithful, always unfaithful. From now on, I am not your girlfriend anymore. Also, our Ye family will no longer have any business dealings with your Zhao family."

Zhang Yunfei was struck like a bolt of lightning, his face full of horror as he fell to his knees in front of Ye Xuerou: "Xue'er, please don't! It's all my fault. Please give me another chance!"

Then, Zhang Yunfei started slapping himself on the left and right cheeks.

But Ye Xuerou remained indifferent to all this, telling her bodyguards: "Let's go!"

"Yes, Miss," the bodyguards replied.

Ye Xuerou led all her bodyguards away confidently.

Zhang Yunfei collapsed onto the floor, his face pale, murmuring: "It's over! It's over!"

Then, with a lost soul, Zhang Yunfei came to his senses and hurried after them.

The room fell silent, leaving only Li Siyu alone, holding her swollen cheek, crying bitterly.

"I've been pretending to be pure, gentle, and modest for so long. I finally caught Zhang Yunfei's attention. I thought I could find a wealthy boyfriend. Why did it turn out like this?"

"I won't give up. Can I only end up with Lin Feng, that poor guy?"

"Ughhh..."

Seeing this, Lin Feng finally understood why Li Siyu had always been so simple and pure in front of him, unlike other girls who were obsessed with wealth or material things.

It turned out she had been pretending all along, just to stand out among the gold-diggers and attract a wealthy guy like Zhang Yunfei.

Her scheming was deep indeed.

But now, seeing that this scheming woman had been discarded and suffered the consequences of her own actions,

And also seeing that Zhang Yunfei had been dumped by Ye Xuerou, with the Zhao family about to face retaliation from the Ye family, suffering great losses,

Lin Feng felt a sigh of relief. The revenge he sought against Li Siyu's betrayal and Zhang Yunfei's humiliation was finally achieved.

The scene at the Hilton Hotel faded from Lin Feng's eyes as he returned to reality.

The hotel room was still the same, everything unchanged, making him feel like he had just dreamed it all.

But what he had seen and experienced was real, not a dream.

And because of that mysterious old man and his single pill, he now possessed incredible supernatural abilities.

Suddenly, Lin Feng's eyelid twitched as if something were about to happen.

At this moment, his pupils contracted slightly, and then he seemed to see some extremely serious events that were unknown to others.

Lin Feng's expression became solemn. He muttered to himself: "I didn't expect Zhang Yunfei to want to commit murder for money. It won't do, human lives are precious. I can't stand by idly..."
