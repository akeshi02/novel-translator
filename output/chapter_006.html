Chapter 6

Fishing and Saving a Life

Around nine o'clock in the morning, sunlight shone over Jiangnan City's seaside area. There were a number of seaside villas here.

Jiangnan City was a coastal city, and seaside villas near the beach were especially popular among the rich because they could enjoy the sea view, soft sand beaches, etc.

Inside one large seaside villa, a young and beautiful woman walked out.

This woman was Ye Xuerou.

Ye Xuerou wore a white hat, had an elegant and clean nose with sunglasses on it, wore a white fashion T-shirt, jeans shorts, two long straight legs, and white running shoes on her feet.

She fully displayed her youth, sunshine, and sexy figure.

In her hand she held a small dog that was completely white, like a cute fluffy ball - a poodle.

This little dog had two black, round, bright eyes, which were very adorable.

This was Ye Xuerou's beloved pet dog, named "Xiao Xueqiu."

Ye Xuerou did not allow her bodyguards to follow and went out alone to walk the dog.

She walked along the seaside road, leisurely walking with the dog.

Although she had experienced an unpleasant event last night, it seemed that Ye Xuerou was not greatly affected by it.

In fact, a man like Zhao Yunfei was not worth her worrying or feeling sad about.

While holding the small dog, Ye Xuerou slowly walked along the seaside road.

After walking several hundred meters, she saw a young man lying on a rock nearby. He had pulled his hat down to cover his eyes and half of his face, with one hand holding a fishing rod for fishing.

Seeing this, Ye Xuerou murmured to herself: "Is this person here to fish or to sleep?"

It was strange that he could catch any fish like this.

However, at this moment, the young man lying on the rock suddenly took off the hat covering his face and revealed Lin Feng's face. He immediately sat up and happily shouted, "Finally, there is a fish biting the hook!"

Lin Feng quickly reeled in the line, and saw a fish weighing more than one catty being pulled out of the sea.

He removed the fish from the hook and threw it into a small bucket nearby.

Then Lin Feng smiled at the corner of his lips and said to Ye Xuerou who was standing not far away on the roadside: "Who said that fishing couldn't be combined with sleeping? I'm doing both!"

After saying this, Lin Feng no longer looked at Ye Xuerou. He quickly took bait again for the hook and threw it back into the sea.

Then he lay down again on the rock and covered his face with the hat to block out the sunlight.

Seeing this, Ye Xuerou couldn't help but smile: This person was really interesting!

It was unexpected that one could catch fish like this. It was interesting.

Ye Xuerou did not stay any longer and continued walking with her little dog.

After hearing Ye Xuerou's footsteps fading away, Lin Feng took off the hat covering his face and sat up.

He looked at Ye Xuerou's retreating figure and thought in his mind: The time is about right. Zhao Yunfei was going to move against Ye Xuerou soon.

He picked up the fishing rod, put on the hat, and walked toward the direction where Ye Xuerou had gone.

The seaside road was very quiet, occasionally seeing high-end luxury cars coming and leaving from the seaside villa area. There were not many pedestrians on the road.

Trees grew along the roadside, with green leaves. Sunlight passed through the branches, creating a starry light effect. The branches between them jumped around with sparrows' figures, chirping happily.

This made Ye Xuerou's mood unusually good. She said to her cute poodle beside her, who looked like a small fluffy ball: "Xiao Xueqiu, say hello to the sparrows on the tree."

The little Xiao Xueqiu looked at its smiling owner with big black eyes, as if it understood what the owner had said, and then barked several times at the sparrows on the tree.

With these enthusiastic greetings from Xiao Xueqiu, the sparrows immediately chirped and flew away.

Seeing this, Ye Xuerou couldn't help but laugh out loud. She said to her cute little Xiao Xueqiu: "Xiao Xueqiu, you scared off all the sparrows on the tree. Next time when you greet others, remember to be gentle."

"Wang Wang Wang..." Xiao Xueqiu raised its small head and barked a few times at its owner.

It seemed to protest against the owner's comment that it wasn't gentle enough.

After playing around with the dog for a while, she continued walking forward.

At a turning point on the road, Ye Xuerou, who was holding Xiao Xueqiu, suddenly saw a man wearing a hat, sunglasses, and a mask behind a big tree.

Before Ye Xuerou could react, this man in the hat, sunglasses, and mask grabbed her arm and pulled her to the back of the tree.

Ye Xuerou was pressed against the tree trunk by his hand clamping her throat, making it impossible for her to call out or move.

Helpless, she saw her little Xiao Xueqiu on the ground. Her hand loosened the dog leash.

Xiao Xueqiu looked at its owner's painful face with big black eyes and then quickly ran away.

Seeing that Xiao Xueqiu was running in the direction of her seaside villa, Ye Xuerou silently prayed in her heart: Hope that Xiao Xueqiu can bring the bodyguards in the villa in time. That way, she would be saved.

"You... who are you? What do you want to do?" Although her throat was being clamped by his hand, Ye Xuerou could still manage to emit a weak and hoarse voice.

"Ye Xuerou, you and your Ye family are really ruthless. You announced the cut-off of all business relations with the Ye family, putting Zhao Company in a dead end. Now I have also been expelled from Zhao Family by my grandfather. Today, I will make you experience the feeling of living in misery."

The man in the hat, sunglasses, and mask spoke with a low and resentful voice.

"You are Zhao Yunfei. You... how could you be here?" Ye Xuerou immediately recognized Zhao Yunfei's voice, and she was both angry and surprised.

She said this because she had a seaside villa here. Except for family members, she hadn't told Zhao Yunfei or anyone else about it.

Zhao Yunfei sneered: "I secretly installed a tracker on your Ferrari over a month ago. I not only knew you had a seaside villa here but also that you liked to walk the dog along this seaside road."

Ye Xuerou finally understood why Zhao Yunfei was hiding behind the tree. It seemed her movements had already been tracked.

"Zhao Yunfei, what do you want?" Although Ye Xuerou was being held hostage by Zhao Yunfei, she had regained her calm composure.

Zhao Yunfei took off his sunglasses with the other hand and looked at Ye Xuerou with a fierce gaze: "First, I want your Ye family to agree to resume business relations with Zhao Family. And you must sign a long-term cooperation agreement between the two families."

"Second, transfer 500 million yuan into my Ruisi Bank account as a guarantee for the cooperation between Zhao and Ye families. If the Ye family violates the agreement with Zhao Family, this 500 million will be used as compensation."

Zhao Yunfei proposed these two demands because first, if Zhao Family and Ye Family resume long-term business cooperation, then he could return to the family.

Second, he wanted the Ye Family to transfer 500 million yuan into his Ruisi Bank account. Of course, it wasn't for a guarantee but to save himself a large sum of money. Even if he was expelled from the family, having this huge amount would still give him peace of mind.

Ye Xuerou looked calmly at Zhao Yunfei and said: "I can agree to your two demands, but you need to let go of my hand and let me call my grandfather."

Zhao Yunfei released his grip on Ye Xuerou's throat. At the same time, he took out a knife from his pocket with the other hand and pointed it at Ye Xuerou: "Call your grandfather quickly!"

If Ye Xuerou tried to play tricks, he would make her regret it.

Ye Xuerou took out her phone and started dialing.

Zhao Yunfei watched Ye Xuerou carefully as she made the call. At this moment, a car suddenly drove by on the road outside the tree.

Although there was a big tree blocking the view, it wasn't easy to notice what was happening behind the tree from the passing vehicle.

But during the car's passage, out of instinct, Zhao Yunfei looked at the road outside briefly.

At that moment, Ye Xuerou saw an opportunity she had been waiting for.

"Shua."

She quickly threw her phone at Zhao Yunfei's face.

Although Zhao Yunfei was on guard, he couldn't react in time. His left cheek was hit by the phone, and he let out a "Ah!" of pain.

Meanwhile, Ye Xuerou took advantage of this moment to escape from behind the tree to the road quickly.

"Stupid woman, trying to run away? Not so easy!" Zhao Yunfei ignored his face's pain, enraged, grabbed the knife and chased after her.

Ye Xuerou ran as hard as she could, but her foot slipped on the sand, causing her to fall down with a loud noise.

Zhao Yunfei saw Ye Xuerou fall down, his face twisted in rage: "Stupid woman, dare to deceive me. Today I will send you to hell!"

With his reason completely consumed by anger, Zhao Yunfei raised the knife and stabbed at Ye Xuerou.

Ye Xuerou's face changed dramatically as she shouted: "Help! ~~~"

As Zhao Yunfei's dagger was about to pierce her body, a sharp sound of something breaking through the air came suddenly.

A long rod-like shadow swung towards her, hitting Zhao Yunfei with a loud "Pang!"

A scream of pain, Zhao Yunfei immediately fell like a broken kite, crashing several meters away and unable to get up for a while.
