Chapter 9

I Won the Big Prize

Around 9 PM the next day, Lin Feng sat on the sofa in his hotel room, holding a lottery ticket and watching the live broadcast of the sports lottery results on TV.

On the screen, the host was announcing the winning numbers: "The Super Big Lottery's 20200th issue has been drawn. The front zone winning numbers are 15, 25, 17, 19, and 03; the back zone winning numbers are 02 and 08!"

Lin Feng compared the numbers on his lottery ticket with those announced on TV and found that all seven of the numbers he had purchased matched exactly with the drawn winning numbers.

The TV also revealed the prize money and number of winners for this draw.

After calculating, Lin Feng realized that with a single bet and an additional 12x multiplier, his prize reached 140 million yuan. Even after tax deductions, he would still receive nearly one billion yuan.

This was like a fortune from heaven falling upon him.

He knew well that buying the lottery ticket had only cost him dozens of yuan. Yet, with his supernatural ability to foresee things, he had managed to win such an astronomical amount of money.

The power of this ability brought great benefits, making Lin Feng feel as if gaining wealth was effortless.

After turning off the TV, Lin Feng lay on the sofa, unable to feel relaxed at all. He was completely immersed in joy.

He felt light-headed, almost floating, as if he might fly away.

It wasn't his fault; coming from a small county town with an unprosperous family background, suddenly having billions of wealth was an explosive shock.

Already not acting abnormally or crazily was quite rare.

That night, he had little sleep. With the billion yuan fortune, he wanted to bring his parents and younger sister to Jiangnan City for a better life in the big city.

With this wealth, he could enter the business world, dominate it, and build his own empire.

With this money, women like Li Siyu would no longer look down on him.

In short, his life was no longer limited to just being poor; now he had many options ahead of him.

Lin Feng felt full of ambition and beautiful expectations in his chest.

Of course, the one person he was most grateful to was the old man who gave him the elixir.

Without that old man giving him the elixir, he wouldn't have gained this supernatural ability or won such a huge prize.

But unfortunately, he didn't know where the old man had gone, nor did he even know his name.

He thought about one day finding the old man and thanking him properly.

Lin Feng thought like this for quite some time until he finally fell asleep late into the night.

The next day, Lin Feng woke up around 9:30 AM. He checked out of the hotel.

After leaving the hotel, he had already calmed down a lot from his previous excitement.

He stopped at a small food stall on the street for breakfast and then passed by a toy store, thinking for a while before entering to buy a panda-shaped mask.

He bought this panda mask because people who win big lottery prizes usually appear on TV wearing masks when collecting their prize money. It was for safety and confidentiality.

Then Lin Feng hailed a taxi and went directly to the Jiangnan City Lottery Center.

That day, Lin Feng, wearing the panda mask, appeared on the TVs of thousands of households in Jiangnan City as the lucky winner who had won one billion yuan.

This man in the panda mask naturally became a hot topic of discussion among many citizens on the streets.

"Who is this man wearing the panda mask?"

"What will he do with his billions of yuan prize money?"

"With such a huge amount, he definitely won't work anymore. It would last him for life!"

...

After completing all the procedures at the lottery center and having the prize money transferred to his bank account, Lin Feng walked on the streets and heard many citizens discussing about himself.

He couldn't help but smile slightly. He used to watch people appear on TV wearing masks to collect their prizes, and now he was being discussed by others in the same way.

This role reversal felt quite interesting to him.

He reached into his pocket, where his wallet was, more importantly, the bank card with one billion yuan deposited inside.

In short, Lin Feng had already become a hidden billionaire worth over a billion yuan.

With this wealth, Lin, first thing on his mind was buying a house. He wanted to bring his parents from the countryside to Jiangnan City and also bring his high school sister to study in the big city.

In his heart, family was the most important.

Since he had money now, of course he would make sure his family lived a better life.

Since he intended to buy a house, naturally he needed to learn more about real estate developments.

Lin Feng suddenly thought of someone: his best friend Fan Xiaodong.

His deadbeat buddy and classmate, and also Fan Xiaodong was from Jiangnan City. His family had a company worth several million yuan, making him an affluent youth.

Therefore, Fan Xiaodong knew quite a bit about new real estate developments in Jiangnan City.

So Lin Feng took out his phone and called his deadbeat friend's number.

The call was quickly answered, and the voice on the other end was one of Fan Xiaodong's anxious tone: "Lin Feng, you finally called me. You wouldn't have called me any longer, I would have called you myself."

Lin Feng couldn't help but be curious: "Did you also have something to tell me?"

"You kissed Chen Jiayi two days ago during school dismissal, causing such a big incident. As your brother, could you not worry about your safety?"

Fan Xiaodong said angrily.

Lin Feng realized that the incident of kissing Chen Jiayi had already spread among students at Jiangnan University.

But he didn't care and told his friend on the phone: "I'll tell you about this later. I want to buy a house now. You know more about good real estate developments in Jiangnan City than me, so today you come with me to see some properties."

"Buying a house... Lin Feng, are you joking? The minimum price of property in Jiangnan City is over 10,000 yuan per square meter. Did you really win the lottery?"

Fan Xiaodong said unenthusiastically.

Lin Feng smiled awkwardly: "Haha, I did win the lottery."

"..."

The other side was silent for a while.

A few seconds later, Fan Xiaodong eagerly asked: "Lin Feng, are you really a lottery winner? How much money did you win?"

"Not much, several million yuan," Lin Feng said to prevent his friend from getting too excited, and didn't mention that he had won one billion yuan.

"Several... several million? I mean, this isn't that much?" But it was obvious that Fan Xiaodong was still shocked by the reduced amount of "several million."

"Lin Feng, quickly send me your address. I'm coming over right now!"

"Okay, I'll send you my location via WeChat."

After hanging up the phone, Lin Feng sent his location to Fan Xiaodong through WeChat.

A few minutes later, a powerful white Land Cruiser suddenly stopped in front of Lin Feng with a loud screech.

The car door opened, and a young man with short hair, wearing sunglasses, stepped out. He was slightly overweight, wore a white T-shirt, coffee-colored shorts, and a pair of limited edition sports shoes from a big brand.

He was a somewhat trendy little fat guy.

Seeing Lin Feng, this little fat guy approached him, hit his shoulder with a fist, and smiled happily: "Man, you're really something. Two days without seeing you and you've become a millionaire. From now on, you have to take me along whenever you go out!"

Right, this little fat guy was none other than Lin Feng's good friend Fan Xiaodong.
