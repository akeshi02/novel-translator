Chapter 7

You'll Be Sitting in That Cell for a Long Time!

Ye Xuerou's face turned pale, seeing Zhao Yunfei fall several meters away, groaning in pain and unable to get up.

She had just recovered from her shock, her gaze immediately landing on the person who saved her.

This man wore a hat, held a fishing rod in his hand, and looked calm and composed with an air of solemn righteousness.

Seeing this man, Ye Xuerou was taken aback, because she recognized him as Lin Feng, the one who had been sleeping and fishing on the seaside rocks.

Looking at Lin Feng's fishing rod, Ye Xuerou then recalled the long shadow that had knocked Zhao Yunfei away earlier.

Could it be that Lin Feng used this fishing rod to knock Zhao Yunfei down?

And caused Zhao Yunfei to fall unconscious!

Was Lin Feng a martial artist?

After a while, Ye Xuerou finally came back to her senses and immediately expressed her gratitude to Lin Feng: "Sir, thank you for saving me!"

Lin Feng extended his hand to Ye Xuerou, smiling: "Don't mention it. Are you okay?"

Ye Xuerou shook her head: "I'm fine."

But she still reached out, grabbed Lin Feng's hand, and slowly stood up from the ground.

She was indeed not seriously injured, except for a twisted right ankle and some skin abrasions on her right calf.

The rest of her body was fine.

Not far away, Zhao Yunfei had been severely injured by Lin Feng's fishing rod and couldn't get up for a while. Hearing Lin Feng's voice, he felt very familiar.

When he looked up and saw Lin Feng, he became furious and shouted: "Lin Feng, it's you!"

Ye Xuerou curiously looked at Lin Feng and said: "You're named Lin Feng, and he knows you?"

Lin Feng helped Ye Xuerou sit on the edge of a stone by the road and said: "Yes, I'm called Lin Feng. He knows me, and I know him too. It's just that we didn't expect to meet again so soon, with him doing such despicable things."

Ye Xuerou heard Lin Feng say they were "冤家路窄" (meeting by fate), and she couldn't help but ask: "Do you have a grudge with him?"

Lin Feng nodded: "Yes, I do. But also thank this scum for making me realize what a woman is like."

"Woman?" Ye Xuerou recalled something and said: "Didn't this scum steal your girlfriend?"

Lin Feng frowned, as if recalling painful memories, saying: "Yes, Zhao Yunfei stole my girlfriend yesterday."

"Is your girlfriend named Li Siyu?" Ye Xuerou asked urgently.

Lin Feng looked surprised and asked Ye Xuerou: "How do you know that?"

Ye Xuerou glanced at Zhao Yunfei in the distance and said disdainfully: "This scum was caught in the Hilton Hotel with Li Siyu last night."

Then, she looked at Lin Feng and said: "But I've already avenged you. I made my family company cut off business ties with his family company. Without our products, his company will be waiting for bankruptcy!"

Lin Feng's face showed both surprise and gratitude as he held Ye Xuerou's perfect, delicate hands and said: "Really? Then I'm really grateful to you."

Ye Xuerou didn't expect Lin Feng to be so excited. Her hands were being held by Lin Feng, making her feel a bit embarrassed, but she wasn't angry.

She pulled back her hands from Lin Feng and said to him: "Don't thank me. I was his ex-girlfriend. I'm also a victim like you."

Lin Feng nodded in understanding and said: "Yes, we're both victims. But with my protection, he won't be able to harm you."

Ye Xuerou: ...

Why is their conversation getting more and more towards a romantic relationship?

Ye Xuerou immediately showed her goddess-like dignity and said: "Okay, thank you for saving me."

"Your ankle is twisted. I'll carry you to the hospital." Lin Feng sincerely looked at Ye Xuerou.

Ye Xuerou hesitated slightly and said: "You should call the police first! Let the police arrest this scum."

"This you can be sure of. Before I saved you, I had already called the police. The police will arrive soon," Lin Feng said.

"Also, I used my phone to record the footage of Zhao Yunfei trying to kill you. This video is enough to accuse him of murder."

Ye Xuerou was shocked.

What?

Lin Feng had called the police before saving her?

And he had also recorded Zhao Yunfei's attempt to kill her with his phone?

How could he do that?

In such a short time and under such urgent circumstances, ordinary people couldn't possibly do all three things at once: call the police, record evidence, and save someone.

Ordinary people could only do one of these three things.

But Lin Feng had done all three simultaneously.

One person doing what three people could do.

How could Ye Xuerou not be surprised?

Lin Feng seemed to notice Ye Xuerou's confusion and smiled: "Before I came here, I was using my phone to take pictures of the surroundings. So I just happened to capture this scum trying to kill you. Then I quickly called the police."

Of course, Lin Feng wasn't taking pictures of the surroundings just by chance; he had actually seen Zhao Yunfei's attack on Ye Xuerou yesterday night.

He had foreseen every detail: when Zhao Yunfei would hide behind a tree near the road, when Ye Xuerou would go out to walk her dog, and when she would escape from Zhao Yunfei's grasp.

He knew all of this in advance.

Because he knew Zhao Yunfei intended to harm Ye Xuerou's life, and since Zhao Yunfei was Lin Feng's enemy in some way, whether it was for justice or to deal with his enemy, Lin Feng felt it necessary to do so.

After all, making the enemy suffer also brought him joy.

So he had come to the seaside near Ye Xuerou's beach villa to fish, fishing not only for sea fish but also for Zhao Yunfei, this big fish.

Because he precisely controlled every moment of Ye Xuerou's crisis and each step of the situation's development.

Therefore, before Ye Xuerou was about to face danger, he had already called the police in advance.

He had also calmly recorded Zhao Yunfei's attack and saved Ye Xuerou in time.

Of course, Lin Feng wouldn't say that he had supernatural abilities.

Obviously, after hearing Lin Feng's explanation, Ye Xuerou reluctantly accepted it.

But she still felt a bit unbelievable inside.

However, nearby Zhao Yunfei, upon hearing that Lin Feng had called the police and recorded evidence of his crime, couldn't remain calm anymore. He roared in anger: "Lin Feng, damn it, I'll kill you!"

He struggled to get up but was met with unbearable pain from his broken ribs piercing through his chest.

Then, he screamed in agony as his body fell back to the ground, suffering terribly.

Lin Feng came to Zhao Yunfei's side, knelt down, and smiled at him, speaking in a voice only the two could hear: "Zhao Yunfei, you dared to murder Ye Xuerou, the daughter of the Ye Group. You're going to rot in prison for sure!"

"Lin Feng, I'll definitely kill you! It's definitely your fault that I'm suffering! I'll take revenge!" Zhao Yunfei's eyes were wide open, panting heavily, like a dying animal struggling desperately.

Lin Feng coldly smiled: "A noble少爷 of the Zhao family, reduced to being no better than a dog. What a pity."

Then, Lin Feng stood up and walked toward Ye Xuerou.

"You have an injured ankle; I'll carry you to see a doctor," Lin Feng sincerely said to Ye Xuerou.

Ye Xuerou lowered her eyebrows slightly and whispered: "I don't need you to carry me... I can walk..."

But before she could finish speaking, she was already lifted up in a princess-style carry by two strong arms.

Ye Xuerou looked up, her panicked gaze met Lin Feng's eyes.

Lin Feng gave her a warm smile: "If you don't want to be carried, maybe I can just hold you?"

Ye Xuerou blushed all over: ...

At this moment, Zhao Yunfei's furious scream echoed in the distance: "Ah!~~~"
