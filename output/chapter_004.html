Chapter 4

Hyper Space Ability

Lin Feng muttered in dismay, realizing he had made a big mistake!

He had kissed Chen Jiayi at the school gate, just to vent his frustration.

Who would have thought that one kiss would cause so much trouble?

With Chen Jiayi's noble family background, they wouldn't hesitate to kill him!

Even if her family didn't kill him, there were countless wealthy and powerful young men who pursued Chen Jiayi at Jiangnan University. They wouldn't let him off easily either!

At once, Lin Feng recalled the old man, who had said there was a great romantic situation waiting for him.

And it could be either a romantic fate or a romantic disaster, depending on his destiny.

The old man's prophecy was definitely coming true again!

Only, what awaited Lin Feng was certainly a romantic disaster, not a romantic fortune.

Lin Feng felt depressed.

At this moment, he saw Zhao Feiyun and Li Siyu had finished their meal.

Zhao Feiyun took Li Siyu by the waist into the elevator.

Hotel 30th floor room 302.

The door closed, and the two embraced each other without any restraint.

However, at that very moment, Zhao Feiyun's phone rang.

He immediately stopped his actions, pulled out his phone from his pocket, saw the caller ID, and his expression turned serious instantly.

"Feiyun, who is it?" Li Siyu asked curiously when she noticed Zhao Feiyun stopping.

Zhao Feiy

"An old friend, maybe he has an urgent matter. You go take a shower first," Zhao Feiyun said to her.

Although Li Siyu was curious about who called Zhao Feiyun, she obeyed and went into the bathroom.

Li Siyu didn't know who had called Zhao Feiyun, but Lin Feng could see it clearly.

He saw that Zhao Feiyun's phone had a call from "Ye Xuerou."

After Li Siyu entered the bathroom, Zhao Feiyun answered the call from Ye Xuerou. His expression changed to one of sycophantic flattery: "Xuerou, is there something you need?"

"Feiyun, where are you? Why did it take so long for you to answer?" a woman's dissatisfied voice came through the phone.

"Xuerou, I was at a friend's birthday party. The room was too noisy earlier, and I didn't hear the phone ring," Zhao Feiyun said in a gentle tone.

"What friend's birthday? I'm your girlfriend. Why didn't you bring me along?"

"Xuerou, it's a male-only birthday party for friends. How could I bring you there? What if they fall for you and I have nowhere to cry?" Zhao Feiyun replied smoothly.

"You're just a smooth talker. Alright, I'll let you off today."

"Yes, my goddess."

After hanging up the phone, Zhao Feiyun's expression clearly relaxed.

Lin Feng thought: So Zhao Feiyun already had a girlfriend!

From Zhao Feiyun's behavior on the phone with Ye Xuerou, it was clear that Ye Xuerou had some influence.

Otherwise, Zhao Feiyun wouldn't have sent Li Siyu away just because of her call.

It was obvious he was wary of this woman named Ye Xuerou.

Li Siyu was merely a调剂 for Zhao Feiyun's rich life; she wasn't truly loved by him.

Zhao Feiyun placed his phone on the nightstand and then went into the bathroom.

Lin Feng's gaze fell on Zhao Feiyun's phone on the table, and he thought to himself: If I use Zhao Feiyun's phone to send a message to Ye Xuerou, letting her know what Zhao Feiyun was doing right now, would there be an interesting show?

Thinking this, Lin Feng reached out his hand toward Zhao Feiyun's phone on the table.

But as soon as his hand touched the phone, it felt like the air itself. He couldn't grasp anything.

Although he could see Zhao Feiyun's phone, it wasn't actually in front of him; instead, it was at least ten minutes away in a room at the Hilton Hotel.

Therefore, he couldn't get Zhao Feiyun's phone unless his arm could reach several kilometers or he could break through space to bring it close enough for him to touch.

But that was impossible and violated the laws of physics.

At this moment, a faint white light appeared on his palm, like the gentle glow of moonlight.

Lin Feng knew this was his spiritual energy.

The spiritual energy suddenly appeared, enveloping his hand.

This made Lin Feng think: Could his spiritual energy allow him to take Zhao Feiyun's phone from the table?

With that thought in mind, Lin Feng reached out again toward Zhao Feiyun's phone on the table.

He could see as his palm extended, there was a slight ripple in the air, as if some boundary had been broken. The air around him seemed to have waves of ripples.

In an instant, the spatial distance vanished completely.

Lin Feng's hand filled with spiritual energy managed to pick up the phone on the table.

Lin Feng's eyes brightened; he realized that his spiritual energy could break through space and allow him to take the phone!

This meant Lin Feng had the ability to transcend space.

This discovery made Lin Feng very excited.

After picking up Zhao Feiyun's phone, Lin Feng quickly found Ye Xuerou's phone number.

Then, with a quick idea, he sent her a text message.

After sending the message, Lin Feng deleted it from his phone to leave no trace.

He then placed Zhao Feiyun's phone back on the table as if nothing had happened.

The spiritual energy on his hand disappeared, and the ripples in the air vanished without a trace.

Lin Feng was eager to know how Ye Xuerou would react when she saw that Zhao Feiyun's phone had sent a message.

As soon as this thought formed, Lin Feng's eyes narrowed, and he saw a luxurious villa estate.

From one of the grand villas, a woman with long hair and an imposing presence walked out. She looked fierce, dressed in a red OL outfit with a sexy S-shaped figure, wearing red high heels like a queen.

As she walked, she gave orders to ten bodyguards inside the courtyard: "Everyone, immediately come with me to the Hilton Hotel to deal with that pair of dogs!"

The ten bodyguards were surprised but quickly replied, "Yes, Miss."

Indeed, this strong-willed woman was Ye Xuerou.

She looked fierce because she had received the message Lin Feng sent through Zhao Feiyun's phone.

Ye Xuerou got into a red Ferrari and drove away from the villa estate with several bodyguard cars protecting her.
